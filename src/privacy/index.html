Privacy Policy: The Consultant Toolbox

Effective Date: May 3, 2025

The Consultant Toolbox is an Office Add-in designed to enhance user productivity within Microsoft PowerPoint. This Add-in does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from users.

  • The Consultant Toolbox operates entirely within your Office environment and does not send any data to external servers.
  • No user credentials, files, documents, or metadata are collected or shared.
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
  • The Add-in does not include advertisements, telemetry, or third-party integrations that process user data.
